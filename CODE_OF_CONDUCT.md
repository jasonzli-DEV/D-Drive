Contributor Covenant Code of Conduct (v2.1)

Our pledge

In the interest of fostering an open and welcoming environment, we as contributors and maintainers pledge to make participation in our project and community a harassment-free experience for everyone.

Standards

Examples of behavior that contributes to a positive environment include:
- Using welcoming and inclusive language
- Being respectful of differing viewpoints and experiences
- Showing empathy toward other community members

Unacceptable behavior includes:
- Harassment, intimidation, or exclusionary behavior
- Discriminatory language or imagery
- Sustained disruption of community spaces

Enforcement

Project maintainers are responsible for clarifying and enforcing standards of acceptable behavior. If you witness or experience unacceptable behavior, please report it by opening an issue titled "CODE OF CONDUCT REPORT" or contacting the maintainers via the repository.

Contact

If you need to report a violation, please open a private issue or contact the maintainers through the project GitHub repository.
